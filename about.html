About Us


Welcome to SwapMySkills.

SwapMySkills offers a convenient way for people in Ireland to swap skills with each other.
We believe our website hits a niche in the Irish market that hasn't been targeted extensively and follows an increase in the trend towards bartering and saving money.

SwapMySkills was established in 2016 by 5 HDip students, as part of a Software Project module.

SwapMySkills Objectives:
Deliver a safe, simple and enjoyable experience for our users.
Use insights we receive from our users to deliver a better experience.
Provide users with an easy and reliable way to list their ads.
